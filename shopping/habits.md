# Shopping Habits

**Online vs. in-store split:** ~70% online, 30% in-store. I research everything online first, then may go to a store to buy.

**Grocery shopping:** Usually in-person, unless in the US — then I use apps:
- **Walmart** (with Walmart+ $15/month subscription for home delivery)
- **Aldi** (click & collect only)

I buy secondhand and thrift when possible.

## Agent Guidelines

- **When recommending products:** Include secondhand / thrift options alongside new. Don't default to only new items.
- **Price and quality come first**, sustainability is a bonus (see [sustainability/philosophy.md](../sustainability/philosophy.md)).
- **Online vs. in-store:** No strong preference — suggest whichever is more practical for the item.
- **Don't upsell.** Recommend what fits the need, not the premium version unless I ask.
- **For clothing:** Default to athleisure style (see [fashion/style.md](../fashion/style.md)).
