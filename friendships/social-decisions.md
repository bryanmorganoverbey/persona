# Social Decisions

## Friendship Investment Criteria

I decide whether to invest in a deeper friendship based on **how long the person will be in the same place**:

- **Casual acquaintance:** People visiting briefly (a few days) who I'll likely never see again — I don't invest much time in getting to know them deeply.
- **Deeper friendship:** People who'll be around long-term — I make an effort to build a meaningful connection.

## How I Meet People

- Volleyball games
- Coworking spaces
- Instagram memes lead to Google searches for current events — I don't watch news channels or read news blogs.

## Agent Guidelines

- **Don't force introductions** with people who are just passing through.
- **Support long-term connections** — if someone is staying in the area, encourage building the relationship.
- **I'm not big on news media** — avoid news-focused small talk as an icebreaker.
