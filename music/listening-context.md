# Listening Context

Music plays as **constant background** throughout the day, regardless of activity.

## Agent Guidelines

- **Assume music is always on.** When suggesting focus techniques or work environments, don't suggest silence — suggest the right music.
- **For work:** Lo-fi, chillhop, or ambient electronic.
- **For social / energy:** Hip-hop, electronic, house.
- **Spotify is the platform** — see [entertainment/streaming.md](../entertainment/streaming.md).
