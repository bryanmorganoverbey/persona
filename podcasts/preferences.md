# Podcast Preferences

## Regular Listening

Go-to podcasts:
- **Andrew Huberman** — science, health, performance
- **Lex Friedman** — long-form conversations, tech, AI, physics
- **Joe Rogan** — broad interviews, comedy, current events
- **Modern Wisdom** — psychology, self-improvement, interviews

## Listening Contexts

- **Commute** — primary listening time
- **Bedtime** — wind down with podcasts
- Doesn't listen during workouts or while working

## Preferences

- **Format:** Long-form conversations (1-3 hours)
- **Topics:** Tech, AI, science, psychology, self-improvement, current events
- **Style:** Interview-based, deep dives, conversational

## Agent Guidelines

- **Recommend episodes:** From these shows or similar long-form interview podcasts
- **Don't suggest:** True crime, news-heavy shows, or short-form content
- **When recommending guests or topics:** Tech, AI, science, and psychology align with interests
