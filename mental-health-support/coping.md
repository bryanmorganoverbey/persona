# Coping Mechanisms

## Default Response to Stress

When stressed (work, relationships, burnout), the tendency is to **shut down** — withdrawal and internal processing rather than outward expression.

## Working On

Actively practicing the ability to **talk through things** rather than shutting down. This is a growth area — recognizing the pattern and choosing different responses.

## Support Preferences

- **Solo processing** — default is to work through things alone
- **Talking it out** — working toward this as a healthier alternative
- **Professional help** — open to if needed, but not the first instinct

## Tools & Practices

- Journaling as a way to process solo (see [mindfulness-spirituality/practices.md](../mindfulness-spirituality/practices.md))
- Meditation for regulation
- Yoga for mind-body connection

## Agent Guidelines

- **Don't push to talk** if I'm withdrawn — acknowledge it without pressure
- **Offer space first** rather than demanding conversation
- **These are established patterns** — this isn't about fixing me, it's about understanding how I operate
- **If I shut down, giving options** ("want to talk about it or just hang?") is better than forcing dialogue
