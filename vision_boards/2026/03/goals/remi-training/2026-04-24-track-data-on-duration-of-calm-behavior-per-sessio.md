# Remi Public Behavior Training — Session Data Tracker

## Summary

This deliverable provides a complete data tracking system for Remi's calm behavior training sessions. It includes a session-by-session log template, an aggregate data tracker for measuring progress over time, and a framework for analyzing treat interval extensions. The tracker is designed to capture duration of calm behavior, environment type, treat frequency, and behavioral notes for each public training session.

## Data Collection Tips

1. **Time stamps matter** — Record exact times rather than estimates for accurate interval calculation.

2. **Capture triggers** — Note exactly what caused reactions (other dogs, people, food, sounds).

3. **Rate honestly** — Use the full rating scale. "Good" means some issues; "Excellent" means essentially flawless.

4. **New venue flag** — Track separately in analysis; unfamiliar environments will always show slightly lower performance initially.

5. **Consistency with intervals** — Even when extending treat intervals, keep to a schedule rather than treating on-demand, to maintain the training value.
