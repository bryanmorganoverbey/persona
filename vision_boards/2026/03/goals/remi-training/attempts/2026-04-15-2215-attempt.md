### Data Collection Guidelines

**For Accurate Duration Tracking:**

1. **Use a timer** - Start timer when "get under" command is given and Remi is settled
2. **Stop timer** at first sign of restlessness (barking, whining, standing up)
3. **Record total calm duration** - not total session time
4. **Note interruptions** - if calm broken but regained, track segments separately

**Recommended Tracking Frequency:**
- Every session in the beginning
- Weekly summary after consistent progress
- Monthly trend analysis to identify patterns

**Key Metrics to Monitor:**
| Metric | Target Progress |
|--------|-----------------|
| Duration under chair | Increase from 5 min → 15+ min |
| Treat interval | Extend from 1-2 min → 5-10 min |
| Incident frequency | Reduce from multiple → zero |
| Location versatility | Expand from familiar → new environments |
