### Training Protocol: "Get Under"

**Phase 1: Foundation (Weeks 1-2)**

| Step | Action | Treat Timing |
|------|--------|---------------|
| 1 | Enter cafe, give "get under" command | Immediately after compliance |
| 2 | Guide under chair/table | Treat when in position |
| 3 | Initial settle (sit/lie down) | Treat calm posture |
| 4 | First 30 seconds calm | Treat |
| 5 | 1 minute calm | Treat |

**Treat Interval Progression:**
- Start: Every 30 seconds
- Week 1 target: Every 60 seconds
- Week 2 target: Every 90 seconds
- Gradual extension based on reliability

**Phase 2: Reliability Building (Weeks 3-4)**

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Enter → "get under" → no treat until settled |
| 2 | Extend to 2-minute intervals |
| 3 | Add mild distractions (server approach, other customers) |
| 4 | Practice 30-45 minute sessions |

---

### Success Criteria for "Consistent"

**Remi has achieved consistent calm behavior when:**
- [ ] 90%+ calm in 30-minute session at familiar cafe
- [ ] No barking/whining in last 5+ sessions
- [ ] Settles within 30 seconds of "get under"
- [ ] Remains calm when people walk by
- [ ] Tolerates brief distractions without reaction

---

### Troubleshooting Guide

| Problem | Likely Cause | Solution |
|---------|--------------|----------|
| Won't settle under table | Over-stimulated | Practice "settle" command at home first |
| Barks at servers | Guarding/protecting | Desensitize with distance + treats |
| Restless/pacing | Needs more exercise before | Add pre-cafe walk/play session |
| Loses focus | Too hungry/too full | Time sessions 1-2 hours post-meal |
| Reacts to other dogs | High value distraction | Increase distance, use higher value treats |
