## 4. Treat Interval Progression Guide

### Phase 1: Initial Training (Weeks 1-2)
- **Treat interval:** Every 30 seconds
- **Goal:** Establish reliable "get under" behavior
- **Criteria to advance:** 90%+ success rate over 5+ sessions

### Phase 2: Interval Extension (Weeks 3-4)
- **Treat interval:** Every 60 seconds
- **Goal:** Build duration tolerance
- **Criteria to advance:** 90%+ success rate with minimal incidents

### Phase 3: Intermediate (Weeks 5-6)
- **Treat interval:** Every 90 seconds
- **Goal:** Increase stamina for longer sessions
- **Criteria to advance:** Consistent calm behavior for 10+ minutes

### Phase 4: Advanced (Weeks 7-8)
- **Treat interval:** Every 2 minutes
- **Goal:** Prepare for full restaurant meal durations
- **Criteria to advance:** 95%+ success rate in varied environments

### Phase 5: Maintenance
- **Treat interval:** Variable (every 2-5 minutes based on environment)
- **Goal:** Maintain reliable behavior with minimal treats

---

## 5. Data Analysis Guidelines

### Key Metrics to Track

1. **Duration Trend** — Is the maximum calm duration increasing over time?
2. **Success Rate** — What percentage of sessions have zero incidents?
3. **Treat Efficiency** — Are you needing fewer treats per session?
4. **Environment Consistency** — How does behavior differ in familiar vs. new locations?

### Signs of Progress

- ✅ Max calm duration increasing from session to session
- ✅ Fewer incidents in similar environments
- ✅ Ability to extend treat intervals without behavior degradation
- ✅ Successful behavior in increasingly challenging environments

### Warning Signs (Needs Adjustment)

- ⚠️ No duration increase over 3+ sessions
- ⚠️ Frequent incidents in specific environment types
- ⚠️ Regression when extending treat intervals
- ⚠️ High stress indicators (panting, pacing, whining)
