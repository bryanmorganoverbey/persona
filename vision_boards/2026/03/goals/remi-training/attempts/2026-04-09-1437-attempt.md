## Measurement Instructions

### How to Measure Duration of Calm Behavior

1. **Start timer** when Remi goes under with "get under" command
2. **Note each interruption** — any noise, movement, or break in calm behavior pauses the timer
3. **Resume timer** when Remi returns to calm state
4. **Record total** — cumulative calm time, not just session length
5. **Track treat intervals** — note time between each treat delivery

### Calm Behavior Criteria
- ✅ Lying/sitting quietly
- ✅ Eyes relaxed/drowsy
- ✅ No vocalization
- ✅ No pawing or pacing
- ✅ Responds to handler cues
