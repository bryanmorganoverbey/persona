### 2. Progress Tracking - Calm Behavior Duration

**Familiar Cafes Target: 20+ minutes of continuous calm behavior**

| Level | Duration | Treat Frequency | Description |
|-------|----------|-----------------|-------------|
| Phase 1 | 0-5 min | Every 30 seconds | Initial learning - high reinforcement |
| Phase 2 | 5-10 min | Every 60 seconds | Building reliability |
| Phase 3 | 10-15 min | Every 90 seconds | Moderate independence |
| Phase 4 | 15-20 min | Every 2 minutes | Near independence |
| Phase 5 | 20+ min | Random/variable | Maintained behavior (intermittent) |

**Success Criteria for "Consistent":** 80% of sessions at a familiar location achieve target duration with zero barking events for 3+ consecutive visits.

### 3. Treatment Interval Extension Protocol

Following variable ratio reinforcement best practices:

1. **Establish Baseline:** Document current treat frequency at familiar cafe
2. **Gradual Increase:** Extend interval by 15-30 seconds per session
3. **Randomization:** Introduce variable timing (not perfectly predictable)
4. **Partial Fade:** Transition from every-reward to intermittent (3:1 ratio → 5:1 → variable)
5. **Extinction Bursts:** Expect temporary regression; stay consistent
6. **Maintenance:** Once behavior is reliable, use verbal praise + occasional treats

**Key Principle:** Never completely remove food rewards abruptly—transition to混合 rewards (praise + occasional treat).

### 4. New Environment Testing Protocol

When introducing Remi to unfamiliar restaurants:

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Pre-visit: Walk past location (no training) to assess reactivity |
| 2 | First visit: Peak hours avoided; bring high-value treats |
| 3 | Expect regression: Treat at shorter intervals initially |
| 4 | Pair with familiar element: Bring Remi's mat or familiar item |
| 5 | Scale: 5-minute target first visit, build from there |
| 6 | Don't rush: Multiple shorter visits > one long failed session |

**Warning Signs to End Session Early:**
- Persistent pacing
- Excessive panting/whining
- Attempting to leave the under-table area
- Alert/stare behavior at stimuli

For each cafe visit:

| Phase | Duration | Actions |
|-------|----------|---------|
| Arrival | 2-3 min | Allow sniff exploration, settle dog |
| Command | 1 min | "Get under" - guide to under-chair position |
