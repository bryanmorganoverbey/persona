### Training Methodology & Best Practices

Based on research from wikiHow Pet and Kinship (2025), here are the key methodologies that complement Remi's existing "get under" command training:

#### 1. Pre-Cafe Preparation
- **Exercise first**: Take Remi for a 30-60 minute walk or play session before arriving at the cafe. A tired dog is a well-behaved dog.
- **Mental warm-up**: Do a minute or two of leash walking and "stay" practice before heading out to mentally tire Remi and reinforce expectations.
- **Bring the right supplies**: High-value treats, a portable water bowl, and calming chew toys (Kong, bully sticks).

#### 2. At the Cafe - Setting Up for Success
- **Choose the right spot**: Sit in a quiet corner, away from high traffic areas. Face Remi away from the entrance to reduce anxiety about people coming and going.
- **Use the "get under" command**: Remi goes under the chair/table as practiced.
- **Initial treat frequency**: Every 30-60 seconds for the first 5-10 minutes, then gradually extend.

#### 3. Treat Interval Progression (per your method)
Based on your established approach:
- **Phase 1 (Week 1-2)**: Treat every 30 seconds to reinforce the "get under" behavior
- **Phase 2 (Week 2-3)**: Extend to every 60 seconds
- **Phase 3 (Week 3-4)**: Extend to every 90 seconds to 2 minutes
- **Maintenance**: Work toward 5+ minute intervals while maintaining calm behavior

#### 4. Recognition of Calm Behavior
Reward Remi for:
- Lying quietly under the table
- Not barking or making noise
- Making eye contact with you voluntarily
- Settling into a relaxed posture (lying down, head on paws)

### Troubleshooting Common Issues

| Problem | Likely Cause | Solution |
|---------|--------------|----------|
| Won't get under | Distracted, nervous | Practice at home first, reduce distractions |
| Restless/pacing | Not tired enough | Increase pre-visit exercise |
| Barking at people | Overstimulated | Move to quieter spot, increase treats for quiet |
| Begging for food | Treats not frequent enough | Give more frequent treats initially |
| Paws on lap/table | Boundary not set | Firm "off" command, redirect to lying down |
