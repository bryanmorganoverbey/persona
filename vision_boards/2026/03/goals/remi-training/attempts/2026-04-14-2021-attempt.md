#### Progress Tracking Metrics

**Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) to Track:**

1. **Calm Duration Trend** — Are sessions getting longer while maintaining calm behavior?
2. **Treat Interval Progression** — How quickly can treats be spaced out?
3. **Distraction Tolerance** — How does behavior change in high-stimulation environments?
4. **Success Rate** — Percentage of sessions rated 4 or higher

#### Data Collection Best Practices

1. **Start/stop timer** when Remi is settled under chair/table
2. **Record immediately** after session while details are fresh
3. **Note environmental factors**: time of day, crowd size, noise level
4. **Track new vs. familiar** locations separately — progress may vary
5. **Monthly review** to identify trends and adjust training approach

---

| Level | Calm Duration | Description |

| Intermediate | 20-40 min | Standard meal duration |
