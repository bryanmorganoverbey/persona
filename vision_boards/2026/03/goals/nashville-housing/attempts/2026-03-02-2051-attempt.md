### 🔑 Key Questions to Ask Landlords About Pet Policy

When confirming pet policies for your top picks, ask these specific questions:

1. **Breed Restrictions**
   - "What breeds are restricted/ prohibited?"
   - "Is there a mix-breed evaluation process?"
   - "Are there any breed-specific insurance requirements?"

2. **Size/Weight Limits**
   - "What is the maximum weight limit?"
   - "Is this weight limit per pet or total?"
   - "Do you weigh the pet at move-in or estimate?"

3. **Fees & Deposits**
   - "What is the refundable pet deposit?"
   - "What is the non-refundable pet fee?"
   - "Is the pet deposit separate from the security deposit?"
   - "Are there any one-time move-in pet fees?"

4. **Monthly Costs**
   - "What is the monthly pet rent per pet?"
   - "Is this per month, per pet?"

5. **Pet Screening**
   - "Do you use PetScreening.com?"
   - "What information do you need about my dog (Remi)?"

6. **Number of Pets**
   - "How many pets are allowed?"
   - "Do you allow both cats and dogs?"

7. **Special Accommodations**
   - "Do you accommodate emotional support animals?"
   - "What documentation is needed for service animals?"

8. **Yard/Outdoor Access**
   - "Is there a fenced yard?"
   - "Are there breed restrictions for ground floor vs. upper units?"

### ⚠️ Tennessee-Specific Pet Law Notes

- Landlords in Tennessee can charge additional fees/deposits for pets
- They cannot impose restrictions that violate rights of tenants with qualified service animals
- Emotional support animals (ESAs) may have different protections than regular pets
- ESA documentation may allow waiving pet fees/deposits

---

### 🎯 Pet Policy Red Flags

**Watch out for these warning signs:**
- Breed restrictions that include common breeds
- Very low weight limits (under 25 lbs)
- High combined fees ($1,000+ total)
- "Aggressive breed" language without specifics
- Refusal to provide written pet policy
- No pet addendum to lease offered

---

### ✅ Pet Policy Green Flags

**Good signs include:**
- No breed restrictions
- No weight limits
- Refundable pet deposit (vs. non-refundable fee)
- Low or no pet rent
- Multiple pets allowed
- Pet washing stations/dog park on property
