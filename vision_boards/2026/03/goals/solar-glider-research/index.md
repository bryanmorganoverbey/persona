# Research Autonomous RC Solar Glider Plane

## Goal

Research components, design options, and feasibility for building an autonomous RC solar glider plane.

## Tasks

- [ ] Research airframe designs suitable for solar panels
- [ ] Identify solar panel options (weight, output, flexibility)
- [ ] Research flight controllers with autonomous capability (ArduPilot, PX4, etc.)
- [ ] Identify motor, ESC, and propulsion requirements
- [ ] Estimate budget and create parts list
- [ ] Design first prototype on paper or in CAD
