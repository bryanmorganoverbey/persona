# Daily Routine

Preferences related to daily structure and habits. Covers morning routines, evening wind-down, and general rhythm of the day.

## Non-Negotiable

Morning coffee. The day doesn't start without it.

## Weekdays

| Time | Activity |
|------|----------|
| 7–9am | Wake up, coffee |
| 9am–5pm | Work (Pomodoro-style) |
| ~10am | Breakfast |
| ~12–1pm | Cook and eat a proper lunch, take a real break |
| After work | Volleyball with friends |
| Evening | Wind down, bed around 11pm–midnight |

## Weekends

Rest and recovery. Weekends are for recharging, not working.
