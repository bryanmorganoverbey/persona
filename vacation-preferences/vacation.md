# Vacation Preferences

## Vacation Style

**Relaxation + Exploration** — vacations should balance downtime with active exploration.

- Not a detail planner; prefers flexibility and spontaneity
- Mix of relaxation and adventure

## Preferred Activities

- **Hiking** — outdoor exploration and nature
- **Beach time** — relaxation and water activities
- **Exploration** — discovering new places rather than staying in one spot

## Planning Approach

- **Flexible/adaptive** — figure it out as you go rather than rigid itinerary
- Avoids over-scheduling vacation time

## Ideal Vacation Components

1. Beach relaxation
2. Hiking/adventure
3. Exploration of new areas
4. Low-stress, spontaneous vibe

## Agent Guidelines

- **When recommending destinations:** Look for places with both beaches and hiking opportunities
- **Avoid:** Highly structured tours, crowded tourist traps, all-business trips
- **Style:** Adventure-seeker who also wants beach chill time
