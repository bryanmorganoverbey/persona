# Property Inspection Checklist

Use this when touring a property in person. Go room by room and check everything.

## before the visit

- [ ] Research the neighborhood crime rate and safety
- [ ] Check internet service providers and speeds available at the address
- [ ] Look up flood/fire/earthquake zone status
- [ ] Review the listing photos vs. current condition
- [ ] Note the distance to groceries, healthcare, transit, coffee shops, social venues

## exterior

- [ ] Foundation: any visible cracks or settling?
- [ ] Roof: condition, material, any sagging or missing tiles/shingles?
- [ ] Walls: cracks, peeling paint, water stains?
- [ ] Windows: sealed properly, no gaps, open and close smoothly?
- [ ] Yard: fenced in? Gate secure? Size and condition?
- [ ] Garden space available?
- [ ] Patio or outdoor sitting area?
- [ ] Parking spaces (US: room for two cars)
- [ ] Street lighting in the area
- [ ] Noise level: stand outside for a few minutes and listen

## entryway

- [ ] Door locks functional and secure?
- [ ] Clear, unobstructed entryway (feng shui: no cramped foyer)
- [ ] Front door does NOT align with back door or staircase (feng shui)

## kitchen

- [ ] Full-size refrigerator with freezer (ice cube maker)
- [ ] Full-size stove with at least two burners
- [ ] Oven
- [ ] Full-size sink
- [ ] Blender
- [ ] Coffee pot
- [ ] Pots, pans, ladles, spoons, forks, knives, full cutlery set
- [ ] Adequate counter space for cooking
- [ ] Kitchen NOT visible from the front door (feng shui)
- [ ] Water pressure and hot water in the kitchen sink

## bathrooms

- [ ] At least one full bathroom (toilet, sink, shower/tub)
- [ ] Water pressure: test the shower and sink
- [ ] Hot water works
- [ ] No mold or mildew
- [ ] Ventilation (window or exhaust fan)
- [ ] Towel count: minimum three per person (six total)
- [ ] Bathroom NOT at the center of the home (feng shui)

## bedrooms

- [ ] Bedroom separated from kitchen and living areas
- [ ] Closet space for two people's clothing
- [ ] Mattress condition: check for bed bugs (seams, corners, headboard)
- [ ] Pillows and blankets: check for bed bugs
- [ ] Commanding position possible for bed placement (feng shui)
- [ ] Natural light from exterior window

## workspace

- [ ] Space for two dedicated desks
- [ ] Electrical outlets near desk areas
- [ ] Adequate lighting for work
- [ ] Internet connection: test actual speed if possible

## living areas

- [ ] Natural light in main living spaces
- [ ] Windows: count and orientation (south-facing preferred)
- [ ] Cross-ventilation possible (feng shui: healthy qi flow)
- [ ] Room dimensions feel livable (no cave-like spaces)

## climate control

- [ ] A/C present and functional (any type: window, mini-split, central)
- [ ] Heating present (if applicable for the region)
- [ ] Thermostat accessible and working

## laundry

- [ ] Washing machine present and functional
- [ ] Dryer present (US) or clothesline/drying rack available (Mexico)

## storage

- [ ] Closet count and size adequate for two people
- [ ] Any additional storage (attic, basement, garage shelving)

## plumbing and electrical

- [ ] Test multiple outlets in each room
- [ ] Light switches all functional
- [ ] No exposed or frayed wiring
- [ ] Water pressure consistent throughout
- [ ] Drains flow freely (kitchen, bathroom)
- [ ] No signs of leaks under sinks

## bugs and pests

- [ ] Inspect all rooms for signs of cockroaches, ants, rodents
- [ ] Check window and door seals -- no gaps
- [ ] Check mattresses, pillows, blankets for bed bugs
- [ ] No palapa roofs or open construction

## overall feel

- [ ] Does the property feel safe?
- [ ] Does the natural light feel adequate?
- [ ] Is the noise level acceptable?
- [ ] Does the layout support good feng shui flow?
- [ ] Can you picture living and working here comfortably as a couple?
