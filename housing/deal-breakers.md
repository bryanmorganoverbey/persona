# Deal-Breakers

Instant disqualifiers. If any of these are present, walk away.

## structural

- [ ] Visible foundation cracks or shifting
- [ ] Sagging or damaged roof
- [ ] Signs of water damage (stains on ceilings/walls, warping, musty smell)
- [ ] Mold or mildew (visible or by smell)
- [ ] Termite damage or active infestation
- [ ] Unsealed walls, roof, floor, or windows (bugs will get in)

## pests

- [ ] Evidence of bed bugs (check mattress seams, pillows, headboard)
- [ ] Active cockroach, ant, or rodent infestation
- [ ] Palapa roof or open-air construction (invites insects)

## safety

- [ ] High-crime neighborhood (verify with local data, not just listings)
- [ ] Located in a flood zone, wildfire zone, or earthquake-prone area without mitigation
- [ ] No functioning locks on doors and windows
- [ ] Property does not feel safe after dark
- [ ] No street lighting in the immediate area

## environment

- [ ] Directly on a highway, major road, or railway
- [ ] Under an airport flight path
- [ ] Adjacent to industrial facilities, landfills, or wastewater treatment
- [ ] Strong or persistent unpleasant odors

## layout and space

- [ ] Under 700 sqft / 65 mÂ²
- [ ] No separation between bedroom and living/kitchen areas
- [ ] No space for two dedicated work desks
- [ ] No fenced yard (houses)

## climate

- [ ] No air conditioning of any kind
- [ ] No heating in cold-climate properties
- [ ] Rooms with no windows or natural light

## internet

- [ ] No broadband available at the address
- [ ] Maximum available speed under 100 Mbps

## legal / administrative

- [ ] HOA with restrictive rules
- [ ] Unresolvable title or ownership disputes
- [ ] Unpermitted additions or renovations that affect livability
- [ ] Landlord unwilling to provide a written lease

## financial

- [ ] Rent or mortgage exceeds budget ($2,000 US / $1,600 Mexico)
- [ ] Excessive deposits with unclear refund terms
- [ ] Hidden fees not disclosed upfront

## yard

- [ ] Include a fenced in yard for a dog. If it doesn't suit a 40 lbs dog it wouldn't suit us.
