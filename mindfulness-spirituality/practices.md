# Practices

Daily contemplative habits:

- **Journaling** — regular practice for self-reflection and processing
- **Meditation** — daily sitting practice
- **Yoga** — daily practice combining physical movement with mindfulness (see [fitness/activities.md](../fitness/activities.md))

## Agent Guidelines

- **Yoga is non-negotiable daily practice** — it's both fitness and mindfulness, so don't treat it as optional or secondary
- **These practices support mental health** — see [mental-health-support/coping.md](../mental-health-support/coping.md)
- **Don't suggest replacing these** with other approaches — these are established habits
- **When recommending activities:** look for yoga studios, not just gyms
